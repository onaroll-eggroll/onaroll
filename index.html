About

Our Egg Rolls

All Natural

"On a Roll" egg rolls are handcrafted only with fresh, whole ingredients. We have made it our calling not to use any preservative or artificial flavouring in any of our product.

In fact, our insistence on not using any unnatural chemical extends even to food colouring; "On a Roll" egg rolls are as au naturel as they get.

Not Your Typical Egg Rolls!
Somewhere between the hard crisp of a biscuit and the soft chewiness of a crepe, there is a happy middle ground where "On a Roll" egg rolls reside.

The unique mouthfeel and texture of a "On a Roll" egg roll, can be best described as a "flaky cake with a slight crisp."

It just might change the way you have always thought about egg rolls!

Lovingly Rolled by Hand...One at a Time
Unlike most baked goods which can be produced en masse on a single tray in an oven, every single of "On a Roll" egg roll is lovingly produced by hand, one at a time, rolled to a specific length and width of our own design.

While it is an extremely time-consuming process, we would not want it other way for our customers.

Our Logo

The inspiration for logo is derived from the idea of a rolling snowball, constantly growing in size as it progresses along its path.

By giving the initial idea of a rolling snowball a more distinctive shape (of an egg) combined with a bright yellow colour (of the yolk), the end result is a blatant representation of a "golden egg rolling and growing".

Our Brand

Based in Singapore, "On a Roll" is founded, managed and manned by two cousins with an aim to provide their fellow countrymen fresh, handcrafted egg rolls.

In the same spirit of providing only the freshest and the most natural egg rolls that we possibly can, we also try to use compostable and recyclable materials in our packaging as much as possible without compromising the quality and freshness in which we deliver our egg rolls to our customers. That is our way of making a product that is not burdensome to our body and our environment.

Heavily inspired by the cigar culture, specifically with its design elements and its celebratory symbolism, "On a Roll" egg rolls are an exquisite choice to reach out for in celebration of life's many successes and victories, big or small.
