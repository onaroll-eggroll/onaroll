The Name

“ON A ROLL”
idiom

definition
Experiencing a particularly successful period, without any setbacks or low points.

phrase
If someone is on a roll, they are having great success which seems likely to continue.

The Logo

The inspiration is drawn from a snowball, constantly growing in size as it rolls along.

The Goal

Based in Singapore, "On a Roll" is founded and managed by two cousins with an aim to provide fresh, handcrafted egg rolls filled with an ever-growing variety of cream fillings, as a delectable and refreshing chilled treat.

Due to the handmade nature of our production, "On a Roll" limits the quantity produced during each baking session to ensure the quality of each individual egg roll.

Inspired heavily by the design element and symbolism of "cigar culture", "On a Roll" egg rolls are an excellent (and healthier) choice to reach out for to celebrate life's many successes and victories, both big and small.
