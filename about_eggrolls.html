Handcrafted Egg Rolls

Our Egg Rolls


All Natural

"On a Roll" egg rolls are handcrafted only with fresh, whole ingredients. We have made it our calling not to use any preservative or artificial flavouring in any of our product.

In fact, our insistence on not using any unnatural chemical extends even to food colouring; "On a Roll" egg rolls are as au naturel as they get.

Not Your Typical Egg Rolls!
Somewhere between the hard crisp of a biscuit and the soft chewiness of a crepe, there is a happy middle ground where "On a Roll" egg rolls reside.

The unique mouthfeel and texture of a "On a Roll" egg roll, can be best described as a "flaky cake with a slight crisp."

This might change the way you look at egg rolls!

We Go To Great Lengths For You
From end to end, each "On a Roll" egg roll measures at an impressive and hefty 190 mm. Combined that with a thickness of our design, a single "On a Roll" egg roll paired with your favourite hot beverage, is the perfect snack anytime of the day.

However, size means nothing if our egg rolls do not taste good.

In that regard, it was never our intention to simply numb our customers with dull, overwhelming sweetness. While "On a Roll" egg rolls are just sweet enough, our customers can savour the fragrance of the top-quality French butter and fresh eggs that we use in our product.

Lovingly Rolled by Hand...One at a Time
Unlike most baked goods which can be produced en masse on a single tray in an oven, egg rolls making is a very laborious job.

Every "On a Roll" egg roll is lovingly produced by hand, one at a time. While it is an extremely time-consuming process, we would not want it any other way for our customers.
