Handcrafted Egg Rolls

Our Egg Rolls


All Natural

"On a Roll" egg rolls are handcrafted only with fresh, whole ingredients. We have made it our calling not to use any preservative or artificial flavouring in any of our product.

In fact, our insistence on not using any unnatural chemical extends even to food colouring; thus denying ourselves of making egg rolls that are easily "Instagrammable".

"On a Roll" egg rolls are as au naturel as they get.


Not Your Typical Egg Rolls!

Without any unnatural ingredients/ chemicals in our egg roll batter, "On a Roll" egg rolls do not have the intense crispness of store-bought egg rolls which keep for months on end.

The mouthfeel and texture of an "On a Roll" egg roll, can be best described as "flaky, almost cakey, with a slight crisp".

In the flavour department, it was never our intention to simply numb our customers with dull, overwhelming sweetness.

While "On a Roll" egg rolls are just sweet enough, our customers can savour the fragrance of the top-quality French butter and fresh eggs that we use in our product.

A good way to describe the taste? Naturally flavoured with mellow sweetness.

This might change the way you look at egg rolls!


Lovingly Rolled by Hand, One at a Time

Unlike most baked goods which can be produced en masse on a single tray in an oven, egg rolls making is a very laborious job.

Every "On a Roll" egg roll is lovingly produced by hand, one at a time.

It is an extremely time-consuming process, but we would not want it any other way for our customers.
