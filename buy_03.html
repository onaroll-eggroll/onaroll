Egg Rolls Care & Information

  • Store your "On a Roll" egg rolls, in an air-tight container/ bag, in a cool environment.
  • "On a Roll" egg rolls are best consumed within 7 days from the date of delivery.
  • If necessary, place "On a Roll" egg rolls in the oven at 120°C for 5 - 8 minutes to freshen up their crispiness.
  • The "On a Roll" Kitchen works with a variety of nuts; all "On a Roll" egg rolls may contain traces of nuts.
