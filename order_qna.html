Caring for Your Egg Rolls

  • Store your "On a Roll" egg rolls in an air-tight container/ bag away, in a cool environment.
  • "On a Roll" egg rolls are best consumed within 7 days from the date of delivery.

Make to Order

"On a Roll" egg rolls are made fresh to orders and delivered the very next day.

Due to the labour intensive nature in their production, we can produce only a limited amount of egg rolls by hand each time, without compromising on the high standard of quality we have set for ourselves and reserved for our valued customers.

How to Order

"On a Roll" egg rolls are available for order on specific delivery dates which are announced on our website and Instagram account (@OnaRoll.EggRoll).

  1. The online order form will be made available in this website one week prior to the delivery date. Orders will be closed once when we have reached the production limit or 2 days before the delivery date.

  2. A confirmation and an invoice will be sent to you via email when we have received your order.

  3. Once payment has been made, a receipt will be issued and on the delivery date, we will deliver our egg rolls right to your doorstep.

Liaising Method

"On a Roll" will remain in contact mainly through emails. We will only contact you through the phone number you have provided only when an issue of great urgency arises.

On the delivery professionals' side, you may or may not be contacted for delivery related issues.

Delivery

We understand that you might want to send friends and families a gift of "On a Roll" egg rolls and time is especially crucial for our freshly made egg rolls, as it is essential to have them delivered as soon as possible to allow the recipients ample time to enjoy our egg rolls within 7 days from the delivery date.

With that in mind, we have made the decision to outsource delivery of our egg rolls to the food delivery professionals for maximum island-wide coverage. For this logistic efficiency, "On a Roll" is charged for every delivery to an address made by the delivery professionals.

As much as we would like to offer free delivery to all of our valued customers, being a small-scale operation, we simply cannot afford to do so. We seek your kind understanding.

As such, to each delivery address:

  • For orders of 1 box, there will be a delivery charge of $5.
  • For orders of 2 boxes and above, delivery will be free-of-charge, absorbed by us.
* For multiple addresses, please let us know through the "Remarks/ Special Instructions" section at the bottom of the form.
